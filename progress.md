# MatraCare AI – Progress Document

## Project Title
MatraCare AI: Real-Time Maternal Risk Prediction and Decision Support System

---

## Problem Statement
Maternal mortality remains a critical global challenge, especially in low-resource settings, where pregnancy-related complications often go undetected due to delayed identification and lack of timely clinical decision support. Existing systems primarily focus on data collection rather than providing real-time risk assessment and actionable guidance.

There is a need for an intelligent, accessible system that can analyze maternal health parameters, identify high-risk conditions early, and support timely decision-making to improve maternal outcomes.

---

## Background & Research Insights
- Approximately 260,000 maternal deaths occur annually worldwide  
- Majority of cases are in low-resource and rural settings  
- Key issue: Delay in identifying and acting on high-risk conditions  
- Limited access to continuous monitoring and expert consultation  

---

## Identified Gap
Existing healthcare systems:
- Focus on data recording and tracking  
- Lack real-time decision support  
- Do not provide instant triage or action recommendations  

This leads to delayed intervention and preventable complications.

---

## Target Users
- Pregnant women  
- ASHA workers  
- Auxiliary Nurse Midwives  
- Nurses and primary healthcare doctors  
- Rural and district-level healthcare teams  

---

## Pain Points
- Late detection of danger signs  
- Lack of instant decision guidance  
- Poor connectivity in rural areas  
- Delayed referral decisions  
- Fragmented health data  

---

## Proposed Solution
MatraCare AI is a real-time maternal risk decision support system that:
- Collects maternal health data  
- Analyzes risk using intelligent logic  
- Provides a risk score and classification  
- Explains contributing risk factors  
- Recommends actionable next steps  
- Enables early intervention and escalation  

---

## Key Features

### 1. Real-Time Risk Prediction
- Classifies risk as:
  - Low  
  - Medium  
  - High  
- Generates a risk score (0–100)

---

### 2. Risk Trend Tracking
- Tracks how risk changes over time  
- Displays visual trend (graph/chart)  
- Helps in early detection of worsening conditions  

---

### 3. Explainable AI
- Breaks down contributing factors:
  - High BP  
  - High sugar  
  - Previous complications  
- Builds trust and transparency  

---

### 4. What-If Scenario Simulator
- Allows users to modify inputs  
- Shows how risk changes  
- Helps understand impact of health improvements  

---

### 5. Smart Action Timeline
- Provides step-by-step guidance:
  - Monitor vitals  
  - Recheck after specific time  
  - Visit doctor  

---

### 6. Emergency Alert System
- Detects high-risk conditions  
- Displays urgent warnings  
- Suggests immediate medical action  

---

### 7. Healthcare Assistance (Optional)
- Suggests nearest healthcare support (basic/static for MVP)

---

## System Modules

### MatraCare Triage
- Accepts inputs:
  - Blood pressure  
  - Blood sugar  
  - Age  
  - Symptoms  
  - Pregnancy history  
- Outputs:
  - Risk score  
  - Risk level  

---

### MatraCare Assist
- Mobile-friendly UI  
- Provides:
  - Alerts  
  - Recommendations  
  - Clear visual feedback  

---

### MatraCare Escalate
- Triggers alerts for high-risk cases  
- Suggests urgent medical action  

---
