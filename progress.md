# MatraCare AI Progress Document

## Project Title
**MatraCare AI: Real-time Maternal Risk Prediction and Decision Support System**

---

## Problem Statement
To develop an AI-based Clinical Decision Support System that analyzes maternal vital signs in real time to identify high-risk pregnancies and trigger timely intervention, reducing deaths caused by decision delays.

---

## Background and Research Insights
- Maternal mortality remains a major global issue with approximately **260,000 deaths annually**  
- Most deaths occur in **low-resource settings**  
- India has improved maternal health outcomes, but challenges still exist  
- Major gap: **Delay in decision-making when identifying high-risk pregnancies**

---

## Identified Gap
- Existing systems focus on:
  - Data collection  
  - Tracking  

- But lack:
  - Real-time clinical decision support  
  - Instant triage  
  - Actionable guidance  

- **Our Goal:** Fill this gap with intelligent decision support

---

## Target Users
- Pregnant women  
- ASHA workers  
- Auxiliary Nurse Midwives  
- Nurses and primary healthcare doctors  
- District-level healthcare teams  

---

## Pain Points
- Late detection of danger signs  
- Lack of instant guidance  
- Poor connectivity in rural areas  
- Delayed referral decisions  
- Fragmented health records  

---

## Proposed Solution
**MatraCare AI** is a real-time triage system that:

- Collects maternal vitals  
- Computes risk level  
- Explains risk factors  
- Suggests next actions  
- Enables instant escalation  

---

## Key Modules

### 1. MatraCare AI Triage
- Accepts inputs:
  - Blood pressure  
  - Heart rate  
  - Symptoms  
  - Pregnancy history  
- Outputs:
  - Risk levels (Low / Medium / High)

---

### 2. MatraCare Assist
- Mobile-friendly interface  
- Designed for frontline workers  
- Provides:
  - Simple alerts  
  - Actionable guidance  

---

### 3. MatraCare Escalate
- Automatically:
  - Triggers referrals  
  - Alerts hospitals/emergency services  

---

## MVP Features
- Input:
  - Blood pressure  
  - Heart rate  
  - Gestational age  
  - Symptoms  
  - Medical history  

- Output:
  - Risk classification (Low / Medium / High)  
  - Explanation of risk reasons  
  - Recommended next steps  
  - Emergency referral alerts for high-risk cases
